The Vision

FREEDOM CON: Rise of the Statesman is more than an event, it is a summons to Christian men to step into biblical statesmanship and fight for the continued freedom of their homes, churches, businesses, and state.

The Reason Behind

Set against America's 250th anniversary and Washington's decades-long slide into tax pressure, overreach, and anti-family policy, this gathering is designed to summon fathers and sons out of cynicism and into courageous action.

Many men feel the same pressure: rising costs, shrinking room to build, and no clear path to fight back. The call of this weekend is simple—stay, stand, and rebuild, so children and grandchildren can thrive in the land they love.

Rise of the Statesman

FREEDOM CON: Rise of the Statesman is a one-time, two-day historic gathering for fathers and sons—an American Congress of Christian men convening at The Gorge on Father's Day weekend. This event celebrates the courageous work of statesmanship that built this nation and calls a new generation into that same work.

Men will gather to worship, repent, and receive a concrete, actionable playbook for rebuilding homes, churches, counties, and state. Out of despair, hope. Out of darkness, light.

Washington State Is Our Valley Forge

Valley Forge forged endurance, unity, and resolve in a hard winter. We believe Washington is facing its own hard winter, and good men are being called to answer with courage instead of retreat.

This is not a call to noise. It is a call to disciplined action—men who fear God, love their families, serve their churches, and carry responsibility in public life.

What This Vision Calls Men To

  • Lead faithfully as fathers, husbands, and sons.
  • Strengthen churches and local communities with courage.
  • Engage civic life with biblical conviction and practical action.
  • Build for generational freedom instead of retreating in fear.

Three Immediate Commitments

  • Show up: gather with men who refuse passivity.
  • Consecrate: prepare your heart in prayer and repentance.
  • Activate: leave with a plan and execute it in your sphere.
“Washington State is our Valley Forge. We will not run. We will rebuild.”
