Why This Year Is Different

1776 — 2026.

FREEDOM CON: Rise of the Statesman is a historic Father’s Day weekend gathering of Christian men at The Gorge Amphitheatre in Washington State—a two-day American Congress of Christian Men designed to awaken fathers and sons to their high calling before God and their responsibility to fight for freedom.

Set within the backdrop of America’s 250th anniversary and Washington’s decades-long slide into tax tyranny, overreach, and anti-family policy, this event will summon thousands of men to worship, repent, and receive a concrete, actionable playbook for rebuilding their homes, churches, counties, and state.

“With national voices, historic stories, and a distinctly Washington-flavored battle cry, FREEDOM CON will call men to stay, stand, and flip this state—not flee it—so their children and grandchildren can thrive in the land they love.”
