The Mission Statement

A Strategic Assembly For Courageous, Coordinated Action

FREEDOM CON is more than a conference, it is a STRATEGIC ASSEMBLY of men from Washington and across the nation to confront tax tyranny, radical legislation, and bureaucratic overreach that threaten to suffocate families, small businesses, and churches.

Through worship, preaching, historic storytelling, side-stage equipping, and a clear playbook for civic engagement and reform, men will be given language for their frustration and specific steps to channel it into COURAGEOUS, COORDINATED ACTION.
