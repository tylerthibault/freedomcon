The Full Picture

Two Days. Outdoors.
Under the Open Sky.

Freedom Con is a two-day, fully outdoor conference held at The Gorge Amphitheatre — one of the most iconic music venues in the country, perched above the Columbia River in George, Washington. Think rolling hills, golden sunsets, and the river as your backdrop for every session.

Camping is the primary accommodation. The Gorge has full on-site campgrounds so you and your crew can arrive the night before, wake up on the grounds, and soak in every moment.
