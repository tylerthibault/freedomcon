The Weekend

Every moment is built with pacing and purpose—precept upon precept, session by session—so the weekend builds rather than scatters.

Four Layers of the Weekend

Four distinct experiences run simultaneously so no man leaves without exactly what he came for.

I

Personal

Prayer guides and consecration moments for men seeking individual encounter with God.

II

ManTown

Vendors, resources, and ministries in an outdoor village of men and families doing life together.

III

Side Stage

Breakout sessions for pastors, businessmen, and civic leaders — tactical equipping for the work back home.

IV

Main Stage

Keynote preaching, worship, statesman stories, and historic commissioning moments for the full congress of men.
