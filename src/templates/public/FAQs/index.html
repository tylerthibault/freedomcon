Freedom Con FAQ’s

The FREEDOM CON is for all men who carry deep convictions about patriotism, faith, family, and freedom, and want to stand and defend those things with honor.

Wenatchee (EAT) is a 45 minute drive; Seattle (SEA) and Spokane (GEG) are both about 2 hours away.

Attendees have two options: 1) Camp at The Gorge, or 2) stay at nearby hotels. See "Accommodations" on the website

If you're coming from the west side of Washington, the Vantage Bridge may have heavy traffic delays due to WSDOT construction. Plan accordingly. Delays of up to 4 hours are possible. Early arrival is a concert-saving strategy. For more info on the Vantage Bridge project, check out WSDOT's website.

General admission parking comes with your ticket and it's first come, first serve. There will be no upgraded parking for this event, but its all free and our parking team will be top notch!
