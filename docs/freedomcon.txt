Press Release
**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**
Stronger Man Nation Announces FREEDOM CON: “Rise of the Statesman” – An
American Congress of Christian Men at The Gorge Amphitheater, Father’s Day
Weekend 2026**
Wenatchee, WA — Stronger Man Nation today announced FREEDOM CON: *Rise of
the Statesman*, a historic Father’s Day weekend gathering of Christian men at The
Gorge Amphitheater in George, Washington, June 19–20, 2026.
Billed as “An American Congress of Christian Men,” the event will summon thousands
of fathers and sons from Washington and across the nation to gather, worship, and
receive a concrete plan to fight for faith, freedom, and the future of their country.
Set in the only state named after a president, FREEDOM CON leverages the symbolism
of America’s 250th anniversary to call men into a new era of biblical statesmanship.
“Washington State is our Valley Forge,” says event host Josh McPherson. “For
decades we’ve endured tax tyranny, weaponized bureaucracy, and anti-family policy.
This weekend is about good men deciding to stay, stand, and flip this state—so our
children and grandchildren can thrive here.”
The two-day experience will feature nationally recognized speakers, powerful worship,
and a rich mix of main-stage sessions, side-stage breakouts, “ManTown” vendor, live
podcast recordings, adventures for boys and dads, and much more designed to
resource men for battle. With a strong emphasis on fathers and sons, FREEDOM CON
will include moments of intergenerational commissioning, practical equipping for boys
and young men, and a call for pastors, businessmen, and civic leaders to take their
place on the front lines of cultural and political reformation.
“In Washington State, many families feel crushed by property taxes, regulations,
unaﬀordable homes, and schools that undermine their values,” McPherson says.
“We’re gathering men to give them language for their frustration, hope for their state,
and a plan of action that is unapologetically Christian, deeply American, and focused
on real change in real counties. Washington is not a lost cause. We will have our home
back.”
The congress of Christian men is open to men from around the country. “For those
men who live in red states, come and here about your future if you don’t fight to keep
what you have. For men in blue states, come and get the playbook to flip your state,”
McPherson says. “And for every man, let’s drive a stake in the ground…we will be the
men we’ve been waiting for. Personal consecration will leave to national reformation. It
starts here, with us, now.”
FREEDOM CON is presented by Stronger Man Nation in partnership with like-minded
ministries, churches, business leaders, and grassroots eﬀorts committed to restoring
liberty, strengthening families, and calling men back into the civic arena. The event will
be the perfect way for men all around our nation to mark and celebrate this historic
250th anniversary of our natin. The event will also highlight strategic national initiatives
such as Vector West University for students, For Liberty & Justice for churches, the
Black Robed Regiment for pastors, and Stronger Boy Way for fathers and sons.
